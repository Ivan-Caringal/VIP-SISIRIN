Project Introduction: SISIRIN VIP – A Virtual Reality Exploration of the Verde Island Passage

SISIRIN VIP (Submerging into Simulated Immersive Reality in Nautical Exploration of the Verde Island Passage) is an innovative virtual reality (VR) experience designed to bring the breathtaking underwater world of the Verde Island Passage to a global audience. Recognized as the center of global shore-fish biodiversity, this marine sanctuary plays a vital role in ecotourism and conservation in the Philippines. However, access to its rich marine ecosystem is often restricted to certified divers, limiting the opportunity for broader public engagement and education.

Our project bridges this gap by developing an interactive VR simulation that allows users to explore the vibrant underwater terrain, encounter diverse marine species, and appreciate the beauty of the Verde Island Passage—all without the need for diving gear. Using advanced 3D modeling, real-world biological data, and immersive gameplay mechanics, SISIRIN VIP provides a realistic, educational, and conservation-focused experience.

By combining photographic references of the protected area with accurate virtual recreations of its flora and fauna, this VR experience promotes alternative tourism, raising awareness about marine biodiversity and conservation efforts. SISIRIN VIP offers a new way to experience the ocean’s wonders—whether for learning, leisure, or advocacy—allowing users to dive into an underwater world from the comfort of their homes.

This project aims to enhance accessibility to marine exploration, support ecotourism initiatives, and inspire a greater commitment to protecting marine environments for future generations.
